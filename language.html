Important things to know!

안녕하세요? (annyeonghaseyo?)
Hello
저기요! (jeogiyo)
Excuse me (to get someone's attention)
다시 말해 주세요. (dasi malhae juseyo.)
Please repeat that.
이해해요. (ihaehaeyo)
I understand.
이해가 안 돼요. (ihaega an dwaeyo)
I do not understand.
감사합니다. (gamsahamnida)
Thank you.
괜찮아요. (gwaenchanayo)
It's okay.
화장실은 어디에 있어요? (hwajangsireun eodie isseoyo?)
Where is the bathroom?
